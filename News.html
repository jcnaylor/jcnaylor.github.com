There's a lot going on right now! First of all, I have just launced my site! Please check in every once in a while to see updates. Other exciting news is that Cal State Monterey Bay's Science Illustration Program is having its annual Illustrating Nature art show! My classmates and I will be having our work on exhibit. It is wonderful to be among such talented individuals, and there will be some spectacular work on display. The show will be up from May 7 - June 4 at the Pacific Grove Natural History Museum. For more information, go to www.scienceillustration.org
